Operating Window

Healthy AM needs a separated carrier, a positive envelope, and a detector that discharges between carrier cycles without smearing the message.

Adding the DC offset keeps the ideal AM envelope positive. Envelope remains above zero.

Carrier cos(omega_c t)

06

RC Detector Output

The detector charges to the envelope peaks and decays between them. Good tau values recover the message without ripple or lag.

What to Watch

  • Stage 3 is the same envelope drawn as the dashed guide in Stage 5.
  • If m rises above 1, the envelope crosses zero and detector distortion follows.
  • If fc drops too close to fm, the carrier no longer looks like a fast wrapper around the message.
  • If tau is too small, the output ripples; if tau is too large, the output drifts behind the message.

Recommended Targets

  • Carrier ratio fc/fm at or above 10x
  • Modulation index m at or below 1.00
  • Detector window 1/fc < tau < 1/fm
